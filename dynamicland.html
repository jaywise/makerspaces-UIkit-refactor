Dynamicland

  • What
  • Where
  • More
  • The room is a computer. Take an object, give it some behavior, and watch the ceiling turn it into a building-wide application. Lights, sound, beads, Play-doh - it's all ready to compute.
  • Dynamicland is located in downtown Oakland in an enormous open attic. What was once a garment space, now exists a living machine.
  • There's a lot going on at this place. If you'd like to schedule a visit or find out more, be sure to check out their website here.
