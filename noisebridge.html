  • Part-event space, part-community, part-hacker closet. The Mission District in San Francisco fuels the creative energy of this heady and clamorous colab. If you find yourself looking for coffee with a latin flair -- and maybe some mechanical engineering expertise -- then this is the place.
  • Noisebridge is located at 2169 Mission Street, a 5200-square-foot commercial space in San Francisco's Mission District
